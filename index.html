Room Types

Our contemporary rooms, suites, and serviced apartments all provide stunning views of the Indian Ocean, Beira Lake, and the city beyond. The color scheme features shades of taupe and turquoise, reflecting the essence of Colombo's coastline. With body-contouring beds dressed in luxurious linens, you can expect a truly relaxing stay.

Basic Room

Hotel Siyaro offers comfortable accommodations for every guest. The Single Room is perfect for solo travelers, the Double Room for couples, and the Twin Room for friends or colleagues. Families and groups can enjoy the Triple Room with mixed beds or the Quad Room with spacious arrangements.

Luxury & Suite Rooms

Experience the finest comfort and elegance in our Luxury & Suite Rooms at Hotel Siyaro. Designed for guests who seek extra space, style, and premium amenities, these rooms offer breathtaking forest views, sophisticated interiors, and top-tier hospitality. Whether you choose a Deluxe Room, Executive Suite, or Presidential Suite, every stay promises relaxation and indulgence in nature’s tranquility.

Specialty Rooms

Experience extra comfort with our specialty rooms at Hotel Siyaro. Whether you're traveling with family, need accessible facilities, or prefer a private villa, we have the perfect space for you. Enjoy a stay tailored to your needs in a peaceful, luxurious setting.
